Supersus

In this game, you will be randomly assigned one of three faction: Spacecrew, Impostor, or Neutral. If you are a Spacecrew, you can win by completing all tasks or by identifying and voting out the Impostors. As an Impostor, your goal is to eliminate everyone by killing them and deploying strategic sabotages to create chaos among the crew. If you are Neutral, your role will have unique objectives to achieve victory. In this game, you can communicate with other players in the match.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • You can use microphone to communicate in-match.
  • The game have a lot of roles and gives role card to increase the chance of getting the role you want.
  • You can switch language to join and play in different regions.
  • You can customize your character.
  • You can play with your friends.
